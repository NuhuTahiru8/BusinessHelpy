About

This app helps brands and companies promote their products, services, and announcements through fast, reliable SMS campaigns.

You can create and manage your Sender ID (brand name), write or reuse templates, send messages to one person or many customers, and track your SMS balance so you always know what you can send.

In the future, the brands that stay “noisy” (consistently visible to customers) will win more attention, trust, and repeat sales. When you communicate at the right time—promotions, reminders, new stock, events, special days—your brand becomes familiar, and familiar brands get chosen first.
